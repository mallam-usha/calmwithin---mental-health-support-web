Basic Mindfulness Meditation

A simple practice to cultivate present-moment awareness.

00:00
Breathe

How to practice Basic Mindfulness:

  1. Find a quiet, comfortable place to sit
  2. Close your eyes or soften your gaze
  3. Bring attention to your natural breathing
  4. When your mind wanders, gently return focus to your breath
  5. Continue for your chosen duration

Benefits:

  • Reduces stress and anxiety
  • Improves focus and concentration
  • Enhances emotional regulation
  • Increases self-awareness

Body Scan Meditation

A practice to develop body awareness and release tension.

00:00
Begin

How to practice Body Scan:

  1. Lie down or sit comfortably
  2. Bring attention to your toes and feet
  3. Gradually move attention up through each body part
  4. Notice sensations without judgment
  5. Release tension as you scan each area
  6. Complete the scan by bringing attention to your whole body

Benefits:

  • Reduces physical tension
  • Improves mind-body connection
  • Helps with pain management
  • Promotes relaxation and better sleep
